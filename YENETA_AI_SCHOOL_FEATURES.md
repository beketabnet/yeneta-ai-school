# Yeneta AI School: Comprehensive Feature & Service Guide

## 2. Teacher Dashboard
**"Empowering Educators with AI Superpowers"**

The Teacher Dashboard is designed to reduce administrative burden and enhance instructional quality.

### Key Features & Services

#### üìù AI-Powered Lesson Planner
-   **Feature:** An intelligent assistant that generates detailed lesson plans.
-   **Service:**
    -   **AI Chapter Assistant:** Extracts content directly from uploaded textbooks (RAG) to auto-populate topics, objectives, and duration.
    -   **Context-Aware:** Adapts plans based on **Resource Constraints** (e.g., "No Internet", "Chalk only") and **Student Readiness** (misconceptions, special needs).
    -   **Teacher's Note Generation:** Automatically creates detailed notes for delivery.
-   **Problem Solved:** Lesson planning is time-consuming and often generic.
-   **Advantage:** Saves hours of work while producing **highly personalized, context-relevant lesson plans** that work in real-world classroom conditions.

#### ‚ö° Quick Grader & Authenticity Checker
-   **Feature:** AI-assisted grading for essays and assignments.
-   **Service:**
    -   **Quick Grader:** Analyzes student submissions against rubrics to suggest grades and feedback.
    -   **Authenticity Checker:** Detects potential plagiarism or AI-generated content to ensure academic integrity.
-   **Problem Solved:** Grading is the biggest bottleneck for teachers, often delaying feedback.
-   **Advantage:** Provides **instant, consistent feedback** to students and maintains high academic standards.

#### üìà Student Insights & Gradebook
-   **Feature:** A dynamic gradebook integrated with performance analytics.
-   **Service:** Tracks individual student progress, highlighting trends and identifying at-risk students.
-   **Advantage:** Enables **targeted support**, allowing teachers to address learning gaps before they become failures.

## 3. Student Dashboard
**"Your Personal 24/7 AI Tutor"**

The Student Dashboard transforms learning from passive consumption to active, supported engagement.

### Key Features & Services

#### ü§ñ AI Tutor ("YENETA")
-   **Feature:** A conversational AI tutor available 24/7.
-   **Service:**
    -   **Curriculum-Aligned (RAG):** Answers questions using *actual* textbooks and curriculum materials, citing sources.
    -   **Multimodal:** Supports voice interactions and image uploads (e.g., "Explain this diagram").

-   **Problem Solved:** Students often get stuck on homework outside of school hours with no one to ask.
-   **Advantage:** Provides **personalized, on-demand tutoring** that keeps students unblocked and motivated.

#### üß™ Practice Labs & Chat Labs

-   **Advantage:** Reinforces learning through **active practice**, bridging the gap between theory and application.

#### üìÖ Course & Assignment Management
-   **Feature:** Centralized view of all courses, assignments, and grades.
-   **Service:** Streamlined submission process with instant confirmation.
-   **Advantage:** Helps students stay **organized and on track**, reducing missed deadlines.

## 4. Parent Dashboard
**"Transparency and Peace of Mind"**

The Parent Dashboard bridges the gap between home and school, keeping parents informed and involved.

### Key Features & Services

#### üìä Academic Performance Dashboard
-   **Feature:** A real-time view of a child's academic standing.
-   **Service:**
    -   **Trend Analysis:** Shows if grades are improving or declining over time (7/30/90 days).
    -   **Performance Alerts:** Automatically notifies parents of low grades or sudden drops in performance.
    -   **Detailed Breakdown:** View performance by subject with clear "Excellent", "Good", or "Needs Improvement" indicators.
-   **Problem Solved:** Parents often find out about academic struggles too late (e.g., at report card time).
-   **Advantage:** Enables **early intervention**, allowing parents to support their child exactly when it's needed.

#### üîî Communication Log & Alerts
-   **Feature:** A direct line to school updates and alerts.
-   **Service:** Aggregates notifications about attendance, behavior, and achievements.
-   **Advantage:** Fosters a **stronger home-school partnership**, ensuring parents are never out of the loop.
